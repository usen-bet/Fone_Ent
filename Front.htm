FAQs

Is FONE on Exchange and what is the value of it?

The value of FONE is determined by the community and is based on the current trading offering. You can see on which exchanges FONE is listed and what is the current value of it HERE

How long do I have to wait to receive my withdraw?

We process withdrawals manually so usually takes up to 72h to receive the payment.

Is there a withdrawal limit?

Yes, there is a minimum limit because Fone covers all transaction fees when processing withdrawals, therefore it is feasible to process transactions above a certain amount. Each App & Game offers the player to activate a VIP status, which lowers the withdrawal limit and activates a number of perks.

Why are all Apps & Games on a separate server?

Crypto Treasures and Fone Network are on one server, Crypto Planet, Crypto Cards, and Green Karma are on separate servers. This is done for security purposes and also to allow for lower withdrawal limits.

I have an issue or need assitance with an App or Game?

Please email us at support@fone.dev and we will assist you as soon as possible. You will receive an automated response with a ticket number and our team will respond within 48h. IMPORTANT: We answer at a first come first serve basis. Sending multiple emails will push you to the end of the wait list and will lead to a slower response.
